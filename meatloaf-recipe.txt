Meatloaf

Total Time: 1 hr 15 min
Prep Time: 15 min
Cooking Time: 1 hr
Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients:

    - 1.5lbs lean ground beef (or turkey)
    - 1 large egg
    - 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
    - 1 medium onion, chopped
    - 3/4 medium green bell pepper, chopped
    - 1 medium celery stalk, chopped
    - 1/2 cup all-natural tomato sauce
    - 1 cup taste the difference morroccan breadcrumbs
    - 1 tsp dijon mustard
    - 1 Tbsp dried parsley
    - salt and pepper to tast
    
Directions:

    1. Preheat oven to 350F
    2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix together.
    3. Place mix in a loaf pan and bake for 50-60 min or until cooked through.
